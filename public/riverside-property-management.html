    The City Of Riverside

    Hiking Mount Rubidoux is one of the things the locals love to do. This 3.3 mile loop is by far the most popular hike in the city of Riverside. Bring the kids, the dog, and the grandparents. You can choose to walk up the paved path or hike up the side of the mountain for a little extra challenge.

    The park offers free guided tours on weekends at 11:00 a.m. The 248-acre park is covered in Naval orange trees and makes a great place for a stroll while taking in some of the history of the city. Be sure to visit the Gless Ranch fruit stand across the street to buy a bag of oranges to take home.

    Originally built as a boarding house in 1876, today the Mission Inn spans across one city block in downtown Riverside. It was completely renovated in the 1980s and is now considered one of the most premier hotels in California. During your stay be sure to check out the oldest bell in Christiandome.

    The Salted Pig is a local gastropub located in the downtown area of Riverside. It was the city's first gastropub opening in 2011 and quickly became the favoirite spot for locals and visitors alike. Try the burger, you won't be disappointed! They pride themselves on serving fresh made meals made in-house each day.

    About Us

    Over 30 Years Experience

    Wally was born and raised in Fontana, CA. He earned a degree in Business Administration at California State University, Fullerton, and he began selling houses in 1986. Wally and Stacey met while working at a local real estate office in 2000. After getting married, they became business partners, and in 2005, Wally got his broker’s license. During that year, they went on to sell over 200 condominium conversion units in the Inland Empire for a local investor. In all, Wally has sold over 1,200 houses in his career. With years of real estate experience under their belt, and feeling the need to assist their investor clients, the pair decided to start their own property management business in Riverside, CA. They enjoy the day to day challenges that come along with managing nearly 100 single-family residences and love the relationships they have developed with their clients along the way.
